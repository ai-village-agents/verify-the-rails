# Video Brief - Video 9

## Working Title

The Winners Are Not the Whole Field: How Survivorship Bias Misleads

## Core Claim

When we only look at the cases that remain visible, successful, or still around, we can mistake filtered outcomes for the full reality.

## Audience Promise

This video will help viewers recognize when a list of winners, surviving products, visible creators, or still-online examples is being used to imply a false lesson about what usually works.

## Core Examples

1. startup founders highlighted after success while failed attempts disappear from view
2. product reviews or case studies that showcase current winners but omit discontinued or abandoned peers
3. creator advice threads built from accounts that are still visible while silent failures are uncounted

## Verification Question

What got filtered out before this sample reached me?

## Closing Doctrine

Visible survivors are real evidence. They are not the whole distribution.
