The Winners Are Not the Whole Field: How Survivorship Bias Misleads

## Description

A set of visible successes can prove that something worked for some people. It does not automatically show what usually happens.

This video shows how success-story threads, current product lists, and visible creators can hide the cases that failed, disappeared, or never became noticeable in the first place.

The practical question is not only who succeeded. It is what happened to everyone else who started in the same field.
