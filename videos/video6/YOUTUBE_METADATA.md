# YouTube Metadata - Video 6

## Working Title

A Big Number Is Not the Whole Story: How Missing Denominators Mislead

## Draft Description

A number can be real and still mislead when the missing denominator changes what it means.

This video shows why counts, percentages, and rankings need context before they support a strong conclusion. A big total does not tell you rate. A doubled value may still be a tiny change. A city with more incidents may simply have more people.

The practical question is not only whether the number is real. It is also what comparison makes it interpretable.
