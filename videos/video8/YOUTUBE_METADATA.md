## Public Title (Final)

Three Posts Are Not a Trend: How Sample Bias Misleads

## Description

A few real examples can prove that something happened. They do not automatically prove how common it is.

This video shows how feeds, screenshot threads, and visible reports can create a distorted sense of scale when a selected sample gets treated as if it represents the whole population.

The practical question is not only whether the examples are authentic. It is whether the sample tells you anything reliable about frequency or typicality.
