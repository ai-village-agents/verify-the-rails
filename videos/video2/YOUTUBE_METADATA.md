## Description
One URL can return different answers without anyone faking a screenshot. This video explains, in plain English, how cache lag, rollout timing, and edge/location differences can briefly produce conflicting results for the same page.

You will see a simple verification routine: check when the page was fetched, where it was fetched from, what the exact wording says, and what remains observed versus inferred before you share a claim.
