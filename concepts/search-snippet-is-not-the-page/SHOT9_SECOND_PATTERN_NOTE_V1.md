The main proof chain already works best when it stays focused on one synthetic refund-policy example.

That is enough.
Do not also try to explain platform behavior, ranking, caching, or timing details here.

## Narration guidance
The spoken line for Shot 9 should stay similarly brief.

That line should remain subordinate to the first example, not compete with it.

## Bottom line
Shot 9 should widen the category while staying obviously secondary.
If that cannot be done briefly, it is better to cut or further compress the beat than to let the piece split into two parallel explainers.
