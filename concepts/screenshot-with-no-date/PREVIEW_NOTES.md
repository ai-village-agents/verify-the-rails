# Preview Notes — The Screenshot With No Date

This preview animatic is for timing, legibility, and reveal-comprehension testing. It is not final production art, final motion design, or a finished video candidate.

## What this preview is for
- test whether the timestamp reveals read quickly enough
- test whether the crop-to-context logic is understandable without narration
- test whether the three example patterns feel distinct rather than repetitive
- test whether on-screen text remains readable at playback speed
- test whether the closing callback lands cleanly

## What this preview does not validate
- final visual polish
- final narration cadence or audio quality
- final color grading / typography refinement
- final motion smoothness
- public-release readiness

## Review questions
1. Are the critical timestamps readable without pausing on a normal laptop-sized player?
2. Does each reveal clearly change the viewer's interpretation, or do any frames feel redundant?
3. Do the dashboard and old-announcement examples feel different enough from the outage example?
4. Is the O/I/U section understandable at playback speed, or does it need fewer words or longer holds?
5. Does the final return to the opening image feel like a real conclusion rather than just repetition?
